## **Q5: Extremely negative reviews (score = 1)**

**Key Insights**

Common issues mentioned in negative reviews include:

* **Late delivery / item not delivered**
* **Wrong product delivered**
* **Poor product quality**
* **Refund delays**
* **Missing items**
* **Damaged goods**

Example comments from dataset (translated meaningfully if needed):

* “Não gostei! Comprei gato por lebre.” (Didn't like it! Wrong product.)
* “Nada de chegar o meu pedido.” (My order didn’t arrive.)
* “Produto muito inferior, mal acabado.” (Low quality, poorly finished.)
* “Pedi reembolso e sem resposta até o momento.” (Requested refund, no response.)

## **Q8: Correlation of review score with delivery delays**

**Key Insights**

* When orders are delivered **after the estimated date**:

  * **Score 1** → 3,554 reviews
  * **Score 2** → 606 reviews
  * **Score 3** → 876 reviews
  * **Score 4** → 954 reviews
  * **Score 5** → 1,711 reviews
* Clear pattern:
  **Late deliveries significantly increase 1-star reviews.**
* Positive reviews still exist, but the proportion of 1-star reviews becomes much higher when delays occur.

**Conclusion:** Delivery performance is directly tied to customer satisfaction.
