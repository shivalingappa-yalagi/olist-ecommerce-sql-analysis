19 – LOGISTICS PERFORMANCE BY STATE

Overview: This analysis evaluates logistics performance across Brazilian
states based on delivery speed, delays, late delivery frequency, and
revenue contribution. It identifies high-performing states and those
needing logistics optimization.

Top States by Order Volume: 1. SP – 42,309 orders | Revenue:
5,770,266.19 2. RJ – 13,004 orders | Revenue: 2,055,690.45 3. MG –
11,804 orders | Revenue: 1,819,277.61 4. RS – 5,544 orders | Revenue:
861,802.40 5. PR – 5,129 orders | Revenue: 781,919.55

Delivery Speed (Avg Delivery Time): Fastest: - SP – 8.72 days - MG –
11.93 days - PR – 11.98 days

Slowest: - RJ – 15.31 days - RS – 15.21 days

Delivery Delays (Negative = Early Delivery): - MG: -13.26 days - RS:
-13.95 days - PR: -13.30 days - RJ: -11.79 days - SP: -11.09 days

All major states deliver earlier than the estimated date, improving
customer satisfaction.

Late Deliveries: - SP: 1,874 late (4.4%) - RJ: 1,573 late (12.0%) - MG:
533 late (4.5%) - RS: 332 late (6.0%) - PR: 207 late (4.0%)

Key Insights: - SP dominates logistics volume and delivers fastest. - RJ
has the most logistics challenges with the highest percentage of late
deliveries. - MG and PR maintain strong delivery reliability. - Early
delivery across all states strengthens customer experience.

Recommendations: 1. Improve RJ logistics with more carrier options and
warehouse optimization. 2. Prioritize RS for logistics optimization due
to slow delivery. 3. Continue strengthening logistics operations in SP,
MG, and PR. 4. Leverage early delivery for customer satisfaction
campaigns.
