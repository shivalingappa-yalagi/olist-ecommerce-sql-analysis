20 – MARKETPLACE GEOGRAPHY ANALYSIS

Overview: This analysis combines customer distribution, seller
distribution, and revenue contribution across Brazilian states to
understand marketplace geography, demand concentration, and supply-side
network strength.

Customers: 41,746 | Sellers: 1,849 2. RJ – Revenue: 2,055,690.45 |

Revenue: 861,802.40 | Orders: 5,544 | Customers: 5,466 | Sellers: 129 5.
PR – Revenue: 781,919.55 | Orders: 5,129 | Customers: 5,045 | Sellers:
349

Customer Distribution: - Southeast dominates with SP, RJ, and MG
contributing ~70% of customers. - SP leads with 41,746 customers, the
nation’s largest demand base.

Seller Distribution: - SP is the largest seller hub with 1,849
sellers. - PR is the second-largest seller base with 349 sellers despite
having fewer customers. - RJ has high demand but low seller supply (only
171 sellers), revealing a marketplace imbalance.

Customer–Seller Balance Insights: - SP: Strong balance of supply &
demand; excellent logistics performance. - RJ: High customer demand but
insufficient sellers → opportunity to recruit more vendors. - MG:
Healthy demand; moderate seller base; good potential for seller
onboarding. - PR: Seller-rich state; functions as a logistics and
fulfillment stronghold. - RS: Medium performance across all metrics.

Revenue per Customer: - SP: 138 - RJ: 160 - MG: 156 - RS: 158 - PR: 155

Customers in RJ, MG, RS, and PR spend more per customer than SP,
indicating high revenue potential in non-SP regions.

Recommendations: 1. Recruit more sellers in high-demand states (RJ, MG)
to balance supply. 2. Enhance SP-based logistics to introduce faster
delivery options. 3. Use PR as a strategic seller & logistics hub for
southern deliveries. 4. Promote targeted marketing campaigns in states
with high spending per customer. 5. Build state-level dashboards for
monitoring customer density, seller availability, and geographic sales
performance.
