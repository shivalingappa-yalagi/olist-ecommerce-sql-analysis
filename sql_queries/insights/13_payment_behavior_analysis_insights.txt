Key Insights: - Credit Card dominates all payment metrics. -
Installments significantly influence purchase behavior (avg installments
= 3.5). - Boleto is the second-highest revenue contributor and is always
a single payment. - Voucher purchases have the lowest ticket size. -
Debit card usage is minimal, likely due to no installment support.

Recommendations: - Promote installment-based purchasing more
aggressively. - Encourage boleto users to shift to credit card EMI by
offering incentives. - Expand voucher programs to drive customer
acquisition. - Improve debit card checkout experience to reduce
friction.
