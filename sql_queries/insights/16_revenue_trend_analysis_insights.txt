16 – REVENUE TREND ANALYSIS

Overview: This analysis reviews monthly revenue trends across the Olist
platform, showing the platform’s growth trajectory, seasonality
patterns, and customer demand over time.

Key Observations: - Major revenue acceleration occurs from early 2017
onward. - Seasonal spikes appear in November (Black Friday) and December
(holiday season). - Continuous growth is seen into 2018 with multiple
months surpassing R$ 1M revenue.

Recommendations: - Increase marketing spend around November and
December. - Focus on scaling logistics to handle peak season demand. -
Use revenue trend forecasting for stock and seller planning. - Encourage
