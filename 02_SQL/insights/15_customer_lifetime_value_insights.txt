Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) – Key Insights

1. High Revenue Customers
   Customers with highest lifetime value.
   These should be targeted for loyalty programs.

2. Repeat Customer Categories
   High Repeat customers generate most lifetime revenue.
   One-time buyers contribute less and require acquisition strategies.

3. Customer Lifetime Days
   Longer lifetime indicates strong retention.
   Short lifetime may suggest drop-off or dissatisfaction.

4. Average Order Value (AOV)
   High AOV customers are more profitable per transaction.
   Useful for premium product targeting.

5. Revenue Concentration
   Often, 10-20% of customers contribute 60-80% of revenue.
   Helps in prioritizing marketing budget.

Overall:
CLV analysis helps improve customer retention,
guides marketing spend, identifies premium customers,
and builds long-term revenue strategies.
