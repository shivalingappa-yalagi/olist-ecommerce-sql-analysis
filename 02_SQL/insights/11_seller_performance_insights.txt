Seller Performance – Key Insights

1. Top Revenue Sellers
   These sellers generate the highest total sales value.
   They should be prioritized for partnerships, promotions,
   and additional visibility.

2. Most Reliable Sellers
   Sellers with high delivered orders and low cancellations.
   Ideal for maintaining customer satisfaction.

3. High Cancellation Sellers
   Sellers with a large number of canceled/unavailable orders.
   These sellers need investigation and performance improvement.

4. Fastest Delivery Sellers
   Average delivery days lower than overall marketplace average.
   Strong candidates for "Fast Delivery" badges.

5. Slowest Delivery Sellers
   Sellers with high delivery time.
   Need process improvement or logistics review.

6. High Average Order Value (AOV)
   Sellers whose products generate high revenue per order.
   Good for premium campaigns.

Overall:
Seller performance directly affects customer experience, 
marketplace quality, and revenue. Monitoring these metrics 
helps reduce cancellations, improve delivery times, and 
increase customer satisfaction.
