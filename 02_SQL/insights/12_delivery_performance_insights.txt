Delivery Performance – Key Insights

1. Delivery Speed by Seller
   Shows which sellers deliver products fastest.
   Sellers with lowest delivery days should be promoted
   for “Fast Shipping” programs.

2. Late Deliveries
   Sellers with a high count of late deliveries need
   operational review or improved logistics support.

3. State-Level Delivery Performance
   Some states receive orders faster or slower depending on:
   - Warehouse distance
   - Courier availability
   - Seller proximity
   - Infrastructure

4. Delay Analysis
   Average delay_days shows how often orders miss estimated dates.
   High delay affects:
   - Customer satisfaction
   - Ratings & reviews
   - Conversion rate

5. Actual vs Estimated Delivery Gap
   A large difference indicates inaccurate forecasting
   in estimated delivery times.

Overall:
Delivery speed and accuracy are critical to customer satisfaction.
Monitoring seller delays and regional logistics performance helps
in improving marketplace trust and retention.
