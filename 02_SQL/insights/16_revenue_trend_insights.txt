Revenue Trend Analysis – Key Insights

1. Monthly Revenue Trends
   Shows seasonality and monthly business performance.
   Helps identify growth months and low-activity periods.

2. Weekly Revenue Patterns
   Weekly revenue helps operational teams plan staffing,
   logistics, and demand forecasting.

3. Daily Revenue Fluctuation
   Useful for detecting sudden spikes or dips in sales.

4. Quarterly Revenue
   Quarterly analysis is essential for business reporting,
   investor updates, and strategic planning.

5. Seasonality Effects
   Some months show stronger sales due to holidays,
   payday cycles, shopping festivals, or seasonal products.

Overall:
Revenue trend analysis helps in capacity planning,
inventory management, marketing spend optimization,
and strategic decision-making.
