12 – DELIVERY PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS

Overview: Delivery performance evaluates actual vs estimated delivery
speed, delays, and seller efficiency. This helps identify reliable
sellers and locate delivery bottlenecks.

Key Metrics (Seller-Level): - avg_delivery_days: Average days taken from
purchase to delivery. - avg_delay_days: Difference between actual and
estimated delivery days (negative = early delivery). - late_deliveries:
Count of orders delivered after estimated date. - total_orders: Number
of fulfilled orders per seller.

Insights: - Several sellers deliver significantly earlier than expected
(avg_delay_days strongly negative). - Seller performance varies widely,
with some completing delivery in 1–2 days. - No late deliveries among
top performers, indicating high reliability. - Early delivery improves
customer satisfaction and review scores.

Recommendations: - Promote fastest and most reliable sellers. - Use
insights to negotiate SLA commitments with slower sellers. - Provide
incentives to sellers consistently delivering ahead of estimated
timelines.
