Customer RFM Segmentation – Olist E-Commerce Dataset

1. Champions  
   - High activity, frequent buyers, highest monetary value.  
   - Contribute the most to revenue.  
   - Ideal for premium campaigns and referral programs.

2. Loyal Customers  
   - Consistent ordering patterns.  
   - Good retention segment.  
   - Offer loyalty points, exclusive discounts.

3. Potential Loyalists  
   - Recently active customers but low order count.  
   - Can be moved to Loyal/Champions with nurturing.

4. Big Spenders  
   - High monetary value but low ordering frequency.  
   - Target with premium and high-value product recommendations.

5. At Risk  
   - Used to buy often but now inactive.  
   - Needs win-back campaigns, personalized offers.

6. Lost Customers  
   - Inactive for a long time.  
   - Minimal contribution to lifetime value.  
   - Useful for churn analysis.

7. Others  
   - Average customers with moderate metrics.  
   - Not strongly segmented.

Overall Impact:
- RFM allows better customer targeting.
- Helps reduce marketing cost by focusing on top-value segments.
- Improves customer retention and lifetime value.
