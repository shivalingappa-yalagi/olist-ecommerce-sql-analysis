18 – CATEGORY SEASONALITY TRENDS

Overview: Category seasonality analysis identifies how product
categories perform month-by-month, revealing demand patterns, revenue
cycles, and category-specific peaks. This helps forecast demand, plan
inventory, and optimize marketing campaigns.

Key Seasonality Patterns:

1.  Q4 Peak (Black Friday + Christmas) Highly seasonal categories with
    strong November–December spikes:

-   relogios_presentes (Watches & Gifts)
-   beleza_saude (Beauty & Health)
-   brinquedos (Toys)
-   informatica_acessorios (Computer Accessories)
-   esporte_lazer (Sports & Leisure)

These categories show 2x–4x revenue jumps during peak shopping months.

2.  Stable, Low-Seasonality Categories Categories with consistent
    monthly demand:

-   cama_mesa_banho (Bed, Bath & Table)
-   moveis_decoracao (Furniture & Decor)
-   utilidades_domesticas (Housewares)
-   telefonia (Telephony)

These categories generate predictable baseline revenue and are ideal for
inventory stability.

3.  Early Year Strength (Jan–Mar) Categories with demand increases early
    in the year:

-   beleza_saude
-   informatica_acessorios
-   esporte_lazer

Likely influenced by New Year goals, back-to-work purchases, and
personal wellness spending.

4.  Niche & Volatile Categories Categories with irregular but high-value
    spikes:

-   agro_industria_e_comercio
-   ferramentas & jardim
-   climatizacao

These categories may reflect occasional B2B or bulk orders.

Example: agro_industria_e_comercio 2017-05 – Revenue: 1,763.74 2017-06 –
Revenue: 1,422.05 Large orders distort average price and demand
occasionally.

Price Seasonality: - Gift categories experience price increases during
peak months. - Electronics show higher average prices during Black
Friday due to premium purchases. - Household items maintain stable
pricing throughout the year.

Seasonality Strength Ranking: Highly Seasonal: - relogios_presentes -
beleza_saude - brinquedos - esporte_lazer - informatica_acessorios

Moderately Seasonal: - cama_mesa_banho - moveis_decoracao -
utilidades_domesticas

Low Seasonality: - agro_industria_e_comercio - climatizacao - bebidas -
ferragem

Recommendations: - Increase stock for gifting and electronics categories
before November–December. - Maintain year-round availability for
essential household categories. - Apply aggressive discounts for slow
months in volatile or niche categories. - Use category-level seasonality
to design calendar-based marketing campaigns. - Avoid excessive
discounting for highly seasonal categories during peak months.
