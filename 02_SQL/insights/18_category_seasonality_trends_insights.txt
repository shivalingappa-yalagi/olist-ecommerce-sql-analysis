Category Seasonality & Trend Analysis – Key Insights

1. Monthly Performance by Category
   Shows which categories perform best in which months.
   Helps identify seasonal demand spikes.

2. Seasonal Categories
   Some categories show strong performance in specific seasons.
   Useful for inventory planning and marketing promotions.

3. Declining Categories
   Categories with reducing orders or revenue over time.
   Indicates areas needing improvement.

4. High-Growth Categories
   Categories with increasing monthly performance.
   Good for expansion strategies.

5. Price Trend by Category
   Average product price changes over time can reveal:
   - premium shifts
   - discounting behavior
   - market competitiveness

6. Revenue Trends
   Monthly category revenue helps forecast:
   - seasonality
   - marketing costs
   - procurement requirements

Overall:
Category seasonality analysis helps optimize supply chain,
marketing strategies, inventory planning, and consumer targeting.
