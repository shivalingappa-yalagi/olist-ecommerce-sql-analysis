Logistics Performance by State – Key Insights

1. State-Level Delivery Speed
   Average delivery days show which states receive orders faster or slower.

2. Delivery Delays
   High delay states need supply chain improvements or better courier partners.

3. Revenue by State
   Helps identify geographic demand and top-performing regions.

4. On-Time vs Late Deliveries
   Useful for evaluating logistics accuracy and reliability.

5. Operational Efficiency
   States with consistently slow deliveries may face:
   - long-distance shipments
   - courier limitations
   - poor infrastructure

6. Customer Experience
   Delivery performance by state directly affects satisfaction and reviews.

Overall:
State-level logistics analysis helps improve fulfillment ops, 
reduce delays, and enhance customer experience.
