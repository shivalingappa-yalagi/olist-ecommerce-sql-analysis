Product Category Performance – Key Insights

1. Top Revenue Categories
   Categories with highest total revenue.
   Important for inventory planning and marketing campaigns.

2. Most Popular Categories
   Based on total order count.
   Shows customer shopping trends and marketplace demand.

3. High-Value Categories
   Categories with higher average product price.
   Good candidates for premium promotions.

4. Fastest vs Slowest Delivery Categories
   Delivery speed varies based on category and product type.
   Slow-delivery categories may need warehouse optimization.

5. Category Review Scores
   Average review score indicates customer satisfaction level.
   Categories with low scores need quality or fulfillment improvement.

6. Freight Impact
   Categories with high freight value may affect conversion rates.

Overall:
Category-level analytics helps companies:
- Optimize inventory
- Improve supply chain efficiency
- Enhance customer satisfaction
- Identify profitable product lines
