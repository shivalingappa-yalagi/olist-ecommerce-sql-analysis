Payment Behavior – Key Insights

1. Most Popular Payment Method
   Shows which payment type dominates (credit card, debit card, boleto, voucher).
   Helps optimize checkout experience.

2. Revenue Contribution by Payment Type
   Reveals which payment methods bring the highest overall revenue.

3. Installment Behavior
   - Number of orders with installments.
   - Average installment count.
   - Relationship between high-value orders and installment usage.

4. High vs Low Value Purchase Trends
   Higher-value transactions usually involve more installments.
   Indicates customer credit behavior.

5. Single-Payment vs Installment Orders
   Useful for credit risk assessment and revenue forecasting.

6. Payment Method Stability
   Some payment types are more reliable (low reversals) and preferred.

Overall:
Payment behavior insights help companies improve checkout design,
offer better payment options, and optimize conversion and credit usage.
