\# 📄 \*\*Insights\_Summary.md\*\*



\### \*Olist Marketplace Data Analysis – End-to-End SQL Project\*



---



\# \*\*📘 01 – Basic Exploration Insights\*\*



\* Total delivered orders, customers, sellers verified

\* Data consistency checks and NULL analysis

\* Confirmed dataset timeline: \*\*2016–2018\*\*

\* Majority of customers are single-purchase buyers

\* Sales and orders show \*\*clear upward trend year over year\*\*



---



\# \*\*📘 02 – Order \& Delivery Performance\*\*



\### Key Metrics:



\* Average delivery time: \*\*12–14 days\*\*

\* Estimated delivery time: \*\*> 23 days\*\*

\* Actual deliveries are \*\*~10 days faster\*\* than estimated



\### Insights:



\* Logistics operations are high-performing

\* Continuous improvement shown across months

\* Delivery delays occur in <10% of orders

\* Fast deliveries contribute to higher review scores



---



\# \*\*📘 03 – Customer Insights\*\*



\* Customer distribution mostly in \*\*Southeast Brazil (SP, RJ, MG)\*\*

\* Repeat customers are <5% of the marketplace

\* Most customers purchase \*\*low-to-mid ticket items\*\*

\* Customer churn rate is extremely high

\* Average purchase value ~ \*\*R$ 150–160\*\*



---



\# \*\*📘 04 – Seller Insights\*\*



\* Sellers concentrated heavily in \*\*SP \& PR\*\*

\* Top sellers contribute disproportionately to revenue

\* High-performing sellers have:



&nbsp; \* Very low cancellation rates

&nbsp; \* Faster delivery times

&nbsp; \* Higher order volumes

\* Long-tail sellers show low activity (1–5 orders)



---



\# \*\*📘 05 – Payment Analysis\*\*



\* \*\*Credit cards\*\* dominate:



&nbsp; \* 74k orders

&nbsp; \* R$ 12M revenue

\* \*\*Installments\*\* used in \*\*> 65%\*\* of credit card transactions

\* Boleto is the second-most used but always single-payment

\* Vouchers are small-ticket and used for promos



---



\# \*\*📘 06 – Product Performance\*\*



\* High-revenue categories:



&nbsp; \* \*\*beauty/health\*\*

&nbsp; \* \*\*bed/bath\*\*

&nbsp; \* \*\*sports\*\*

\* Best-selling product category has moderate prices

\* Freight contributes significantly to revenue in bulky categories

\* High review score categories correlate with fast delivery times



---



\# \*\*📘 07 – Geolocation Insights\*\*



\* Delivery distance to customers varies heavily by state

\* Dense metros have shorter delivery routes

\* Rural \& northern states have long routes and slower deliveries

\* Major customer clusters centered around:



&nbsp; \* São Paulo

&nbsp; \* Rio de Janeiro

&nbsp; \* Belo Horizonte



---



\# \*\*📘 08 – Review Analysis\*\*



\* Overall average review score: \*\*4.08\*\*

\* 1-star reviews: Delivery delays + damaged products

\* 5-star reviews: Fast delivery + product quality

\* Review comments strongly correlated with:



&nbsp; \* Freight delays

&nbsp; \* Seller performance

&nbsp; \* Packaging issues

\# \*\*📘 11 – Seller Performance\*\*

\# \*\*📘 12 – Delivery Performance Analysis\*\*

&nbsp; \* SP: 4–5%

\# \*\*📘 14 – Product Category Performance\*\*

\# \*\*📘 15 – Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)\*\*



\### Key Findings:



\* Top customers generate \*\*R$ 6K–13K\*\* each

\* Almost all are \*\*one-time buyers\*\*

\* Customer lifetime = \*\*0 days\*\* for most (first = last purchase)

\* Extremely low retention

\* CLV can be improved greatly via CRM \& loyalty programs



---



\# \*\*📘 16 – Revenue Trend Analysis\*\*



\### Revenue growth from 2016 → 2018:



\* 2016: very low activity

\* 2017: strong month-on-month growth

\* 2018: multiple \*\*R$ 1M+ months\*\*

\* Q4 shows clear seasonal spikes (Black Friday + Christmas)



---

\# \*\*# 📊 Overall Marketplace Insights\*\*



\### 🔷 Marketplace Strengths



\* Strong geographic demand

\* Large seller network

\* Fast delivery speeds

\* Good review scores overall

\* High AOV in several categories

\* Revenue scalability proven (1M+ per month)



\### 🔶 Marketplace Weaknesses



\* Extremely low retention

\* Few repeat buyers

\* Seasonality-dependent revenue

\* Logistics weaknesses in RJ \& RS

\* Product returns \& negative reviews linked to packaging

\* Seller performance inconsistency
